##  Project Description

The Coca-Cola Retailer Dashboard provides insights into:

- **Total Sales and Units Sold**
- **Average Price per Unit**
- **Operating Profit**
- **Sales & Operating Margin by Quarter**
- **Sales by Region**
- **Top Beverage Brands by Sales**
- **Market Share by Total Revenue**

The dashboard was built using **Microsoft Excel**, leveraging pivot tables, charts, slicers, and conditional formatting to create an interactive and professional report.

## Insights & Recommendations

### ðŸ“ˆ Key Insights

1. **Coca-Cola is the market leader** with the highest units sold (5.9M+) and top market share (23% of total revenue).
2. **Dasani Water and Diet Coke** also perform strongly, contributing 20% and 17% to total revenue respectively.
3. **Operating margins** showed significant improvement in 2023, peaking in Q3 with the highest revenue and profitability.
4. The **West region leads in total sales** ($3.6M+), followed by the Northeast and South regions.
5. **Powerade and Fanta** have lower sales volumes and revenue compared to other brands, but still maintain steady contribution.
6. **Average price per unit** stays around $0.45, with slight variations by brand.

### Recommendations

- **Focus marketing efforts in high-performing regions** (like the West and Northeast) while strategizing on how to boost underperforming regions (e.g., Midwest).
- **Consider bundling or discount promotions for Fanta and Powerade** to drive volume in those categories.
- **Monitor pricing strategy** to ensure profit margins are optimized while maintaining competitiveness.
- **Further analyze seasonal trends** behind Q3 performance spike; possibly tied to summer sales.
- **Engage top retailers like Walmart and Target** with custom promotions to drive mutual growth.
